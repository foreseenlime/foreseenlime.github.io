Onehunga High School robotics

We are SymbiOHSis, the robotics team from Onehunga High School. We were the first team ever team in New Zealand to compete in the Vex Robotics Competition when our school registered over 15 years ago, which lead the way for the other teams in the country.

Our school has managed to win many awards and competitions over the years, such as our school's national competition (multiple years), and even the Vex Robotics World Championship in 2012.
