Auckland Youth Fest

We are a large youth-focused event bringing secondary schools, tertiary students, performers, and community groups together for a one-day festival, including music, performances, stalls, and activities.

Event info

Auckland Youth Fest is a celebration of creativity, culture, and community, bringing together young people from across Auckland for a day of entertainment, connection, and inspiration. The festival provides a platform for students, performers, artists, and community organisations to showcase their talents while encouraging participation and engagement in a welcoming environment.
